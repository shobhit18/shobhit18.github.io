ONLINE EXAMINATION PORTAL

MAJOR PROJECT 2013-14

About The Portal

Our idea is to create a web app of online examination portal. As we know there are many companies who conduct exams for placements and competitive exams like amcat and elitmus.They use a certain examination portal. But I experienced that there online portal is very slow and most of them crash or hang during the exam.

We are going to make an attractive examination portal in which user can create an account after which user have to signin to attempt the test. Most of the currently existing portals we know have some problems like crashing or getting hanged. We will try to overcome these problems. We tried to make this site robust, reliable and it takes less bandwidth to move from one page to next page. We have also working on encryption decryption of the password using some algo.
